Ordering

 

There are a few different ways to purchase Bafilabèn.

To download individual tracks, or the entire album, go to www.digstation.com/tworivers. This is the best way to order for those of you who are outside the U.S.

We'll be up and running on iTunes, etc. soon, but if you have a choice, we recommend DigStation, which offers higher-quality downloads and offers 100% of sales to independent artists (like us!).

To order a hard copy of the album, go to cdbaby.com/cd/tworivers.
